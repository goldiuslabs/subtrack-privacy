# Privacy Policy for Subtrack

**Last updated:** February 2026

Subtrack is a personal finance application designed to help users track recurring subscriptions and upcoming renewals.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the Subtrack application.

Subtrack is developed and maintained by **Goldius Labs**.

---

## Information We Collect

Subtrack does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

---

## SMS Access

Subtrack may request access to SMS messages **only with explicit user consent** in order to detect recurring subscription payments from bank transaction notifications.

SMS access is **user-initiated**:
- SMS messages are scanned **only when the user explicitly enables or triggers scanning in the app**
- Subtrack does **not** scan SMS messages periodically
- Subtrack does **not** scan SMS messages in the background

SMS messages are:
- **Processed locally on the user’s device**
- **Not stored**
- **Not transmitted off the device**
- **Not shared with any third parties**

SMS content is used **in memory only** during processing and is **discarded immediately after detection is completed**.

Only derived subscription metadata (such as merchant name, billing interval, currency, and approximate amount) may be saved locally on the device.

---

## Notifications

Subtrack may request permission to send notifications in order to:
- Notify users about upcoming subscription renewals
- Inform users about detected subscription-related events

Notification permission is used **only to display alerts generated by the app**.  
Notifications do **not** include SMS content and are not used to share data with third parties.

---

## Data Storage

All data generated by Subtrack is stored **locally on the user’s device**.

Subtrack does not operate backend servers and does not upload or sync user data to external services.

---

## Data Sharing

Subtrack does **not** share user data with third parties.

---

## User Control

Users can:
- Enable or disable SMS scanning at any time
- Manually add, edit, or delete subscriptions
- Control notification permissions through system settings
- Remove all locally stored data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

---

## Children’s Privacy

Subtrack is intended for adults and does not target or knowingly collect data from children.
