# Privacy Policy for Subtrack

**Last updated:** February 2026

Subtrack is a personal finance application designed to help users track recurring subscriptions and upcoming renewals.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the Subtrack application.

---

## About the Developer

Subtrack is developed and maintained by **Goldius Labs**.

**goldius.play@gmail.com**

---

## Information We Collect

Subtrack does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

---

## SMS Access

Subtrack may request access to SMS messages **only with explicit user consent** in order to automatically detect recurring subscription payments from bank transaction notifications.

- SMS messages are **processed locally on the user’s device**
- SMS content is **not stored**
- SMS content is **not transmitted off the device**
- SMS content is **not shared with any third parties**

SMS messages are used **in memory only** during processing and are immediately discarded after detection is completed.

Only derived subscription metadata (such as merchant name, billing interval, currency, and approximate amount) may be saved locally on the device.

---

## Data Storage

All data generated by Subtrack is stored **locally on the user’s device**.

Subtrack does not operate backend servers and does not upload or sync user data to external services.

---

## Data Sharing

Subtrack does **not** share user data with third parties.

---

## User Control

Users can:
- Enable or disable SMS scanning at any time
- Manually add, edit, or delete subscriptions
- Remove all locally stored data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

---

## Children’s Privacy

Subtrack is intended for adults and does not target or knowingly collect data from children.
