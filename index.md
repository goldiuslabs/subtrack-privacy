Privacy Policy — Subtrack

Last updated: February 2026

Subtrack is a personal finance application designed to help users track recurring subscriptions and upcoming renewals.

Information We Collect

Subtrack does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

SMS Access

Subtrack may request access to SMS messages only with explicit user consent in order to detect recurring subscription payments from bank transaction notifications.

SMS messages are processed locally on the user’s device

SMS content is not stored

SMS content is not transmitted off the device

SMS content is not shared with any third parties

Only derived subscription metadata (such as merchant name, billing interval, currency, and approximate amount) may be saved locally on the device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the user’s device. Subtrack does not operate backend servers and does not upload user data.

Data Sharing

Subtrack does not share user data with third parties.

User Control

Users can:

Disable SMS scanning at any time

Manually add or delete subscriptions

Clear all app data by uninstalling the app

Children’s Privacy

Subtrack is intended for adults and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements.
